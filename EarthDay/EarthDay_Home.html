Welcome to the modern SNU Earth day page. As of Earth Day 2020, I will create updates for Earth Day on this page, instead of creating new pages.

There are some achievements that have been made to help, such as the event #TeamTrees from 2019. The Arbor Day foundation will plant the 20 million trees this week, but that is not enough to save the world.

#TeamTrees

¬∂

TeamTrees was an event hosted by YouTuber Mr. Beast who. for his 20 million subscriber special, he made a project to have people come together and donate 20 million dollars to plant 20 million trees.

The event was hosted by the Arbor Day foundation. For every dollar donated, 1 tree would be planted.

There is still hope

There are a lot of people who have given up, and don't care that humanity dies with Earth. A lesson we can take from the COVID-19 outbreak is that there is still hope. During the outbreak, factories shut down, and the Earth began to recover significantly.

This pandemic will end, and unfortunately if we don't do something, we are going to go right back to where we were when the factories were going. Stand up, and make a movement for a more green society.

Environmental issues¬∂

Our planet currently is in grave danger due to many issues. These issues include:

Overpopulation

Humans overpopulated Earth. There are not enough resources for the growing billions of people to survive on this planet. One of the safe ways to deal with this issue is to not have a baby. You should really decide if you need a baby, and if you want to raise one in this world.

Deforestation

Rainforests are a beautiful environment with a rare ecosystem that is very difficult to imitate. There are a lot of beautiful animals that live here, along with important resources which are being taken down and destroyed. Over 20% of the worlds rainforests have been destroyed. It will take decades for them to grow back.

Global warming & Climate change

Global warming and climate change are HUGE and SERIOUS issues. Our planets Ozone layer has protected us from the sun for billions of years, but now it is being destroyed. As it is taking more damage, the dangers of the sun come to Earth even stronger. This can make dangerous events occur like forest fires, droughts, and cancer. Would you like to have cancer? Most likely you wouldn't. What would you do if you didn't have any water? You would die!

But what can you do to help? Technology is getting a lot better. Eventually, electric cars will become more accessible for the consumer. So if possible, try to get an electric car. The exhaust from your cars and trucks is destroying the Ozone layer, and once that is gone, Earth will go up in flames. Everything you ever worked for will be burned to nothingness right in front of your eyes. Other than that, don't start fires, don't smoke, don't output too much exhaust (a laptop is completely fine) and be more cautious on what you do

What you can do to help ¬∂

Unfortunately, our beautiful planet is under attack by threats like radiation, global warming, overpopulation, deforestation, fracking, and many other threats. We need to preserve this planet!

Helping can be very simple. If everyone were to pitch in, we could make a big difference.

* For every plant you take down, plant one in its place, along with another plant

* Pick up garbage. This can help

* Don't start any unneccesary fires

* Conserve energy. Turn off the lights when you aren't using them

* Find other ways to help the planet

Live on, Earth!
